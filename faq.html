Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Safe Maternity and how it helps you during your pregnancy journey

üåü General Questions

What is Safe Maternity? ‚ñº

Safe Maternity is a comprehensive pregnancy safety guide that helps expecting and nursing mothers make informed decisions about foods, medications, activities, and products during pregnancy and breastfeeding.

Our AI-powered platform analyzes current medical guidelines, research papers, and trusted health sources to provide instant, evidence-based safety information. We help you answer questions like "Can I eat sushi while pregnant?" or "Is this medication safe for my baby?"

Who should use Safe Maternity? ‚ñº

Safe Maternity is designed for:

  • Pregnant women at any stage of pregnancy
  • Breastfeeding mothers (Premium feature)
  • Partners and family members supporting pregnant women
  • Healthcare professionals seeking quick reference information
  • Women planning to conceive who want to prepare in advance
How does Safe Maternity work? ‚ñº

Safe Maternity uses advanced AI technology to provide instant safety information:

  1. You ask a question - Type or photograph any item you want to check
  2. AI analyzes - Our system searches through thousands of medical sources, guidelines, and research papers
  3. Get clear answers - Receive a comprehensive safety assessment with:
    • Clear safety rating (1-10 scale)
    • Detailed explanation of risks and benefits
    • Practical tips and recommendations
    • Safer alternatives when applicable
Does Safe Maternity replace my doctor? ‚ñº

No, Safe Maternity does not replace professional medical advice.

While we provide evidence-based information from trusted medical sources, every pregnancy is unique. Safe Maternity is designed to:

  • ‚úì Give you quick, reliable information for everyday questions
  • ‚úì Help you make informed decisions
  • ‚úì Prepare better questions for your healthcare provider
  • ‚úì Provide general guidance based on medical consensus

Always consult with your healthcare provider for personalized medical advice, especially for:

  • Prescription medications
  • High-risk pregnancies
  • Pre-existing medical conditions
  • Any concerning symptoms

What do the risk scores mean? ‚ñº

Safe Maternity uses a clear 1-10 risk scale:

  • 1-3 (Safe): Generally safe with little to no known risks. Green indicator.
  • 4-6 (Caution): May be safe with limitations or precautions. Consider alternatives or moderate use. Yellow indicator.
  • 7-10 (Avoid): Known risks to pregnancy/baby. Avoid or consult your healthcare provider immediately. Red indicator.

Each score comes with detailed explanations, research backing, and practical recommendations.

This ensures you have comprehensive guidance throughout your entire motherhood journey.

üîí Safety & Accuracy

Where does Safe Maternity get its information? ‚ñº

Our information comes from the most trusted medical sources:

  • Medical Organizations: American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG), American Pregnancy Association, CDC
  • Research Databases: PubMed, medical journals, peer-reviewed studies
  • Healthcare Institutions: Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic, Johns Hopkins
  • Government Resources: FDA, NIH, WHO guidelines
  • Specialty Databases: LactMed (for breastfeeding), MotherToBaby

Our AI is trained on current medical literature and updated regularly as new research emerges.

How accurate is the information? ‚ñº

Safe Maternity strives for the highest accuracy by:

  • Using only evidence-based medical sources
  • Regular updates as new research is published
  • Cross-referencing multiple authoritative sources
  • Clear indication when evidence is limited or conflicting
  • Doctor review of common queries and high-risk items

However, medical science is constantly evolving, and individual circumstances vary. This is why we always recommend consulting your healthcare provider for personalized advice.

How often is the information updated? ‚ñº

We take data freshness seriously:

  • Daily: Our AI model is updated with new medical publications
  • Weekly: Review of major medical guideline changes
  • Monthly: Comprehensive database refresh
  • Immediate: Critical safety alerts and FDA warnings

This ensures you always have access to the latest pregnancy safety information.

Is my data private and secure? ‚ñº

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority:

  • Encrypted Data: All information is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • No Selling: We never sell your data to third parties
  • HIPAA Compliant: We follow healthcare data protection standards
  • Anonymous Searches: You can use Safe Maternity without an account
  • Data Deletion: Easy account and data deletion at any time
  • Limited Access: Only you can see your search history and pregnancy log

Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.

What if I think the information is wrong? ‚ñº

We take accuracy seriously and welcome feedback:

  1. Use the "Report Issue" button on any safety check result
  2. Provide details about what you believe is incorrect
  3. Include sources if you have them

If confirmed, we'll update the information immediately and notify affected users. Your feedback helps improve Safe Maternity for everyone.
