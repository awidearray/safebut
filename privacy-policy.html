Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Safe Maternity? ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our pregnancy safety checking service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Search Queries: The items, foods, or activities you search for safety information about
  • Uploaded Images: If you choose to upload images for analysis, we process these temporarily
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you use our service to improve functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide pregnancy safety information based on your queries
  • Analyze uploaded images to identify items and provide safety guidance
  • Personalize your experience if you have an account
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Send important updates about our service (with your consent)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Uploaded images are processed temporarily and not permanently stored
  • Search queries are cached temporarily for performance but not linked to personal identities
  • We use secure third-party services for AI processing (Venice AI)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Venice AI: For processing safety queries and image analysis
  • Google OAuth: For optional social login functionality
  • Analytics Services: For anonymous usage statistics

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Use our service anonymously without creating an account

Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Search queries: Cached for up to 1 hour for performance
  • Uploaded images: Processed immediately and deleted after analysis
  • Account information: Until you request deletion
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized indefinitely

Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for expecting parents and those planning pregnancy. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Medical Disclaimer

The information provided by Safe Maternity? is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider for medical decisions during pregnancy.
